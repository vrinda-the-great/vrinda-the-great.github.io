    Numerology Characteristics of November 1st :
    ​

    Some specifications about the product:
    As a Scorpio born on November 1st, you are known for your ambition, passion and outer reserve. You have a passion that defines your inner-self. Your passion materializes as an ambition for success that leaves you unsatisfied being anything but the best. While your friends and family admire your drive, they may be most impressed with your ability to remain calm and collected in any situation. Your outer self is so reserved that others struggle to understand your true intentions at times.

    Work and Finances
    Your natural drive and intellect create a variety of career paths for you to choose from. Your determination and focus could translate to success in the fields of science, business, or law. Similarly, your financial awareness may fit well in a career in sales, banking or promotion. You may take the entrepreneurial route, which was the path of Larry Flynt, who was also born on November 1st. In whichever career path you choose, allow the success of Fernando Valenzuela, another of your celebrity birthday twins, serve as encouragement.

    Emotional Health
    Water is your sign's paired element and of all the zodiac signs, you are the only one to have a fixed relationship to water. You find yourself at home in the deep, mysterious seas of emotions. As you experience emotions deeply, you have developed the ability to be understanding and compassionate. Although the determined, and somewhat stubborn, qualities of a strong river will bring you great success in life, water's influence can also lead to detrimental moodiness.

    Strengths and Weaknesses
    Pluto is the planetary ruler of your sign, but as you were born in the first Decan, or part, of the sign, you actually receive two helpings of Pluto's planetary powers. Pluto is the planet of transformation, power and renewal, which explains for the presence of these qualities in your personality. You will always be driven for power and success. Although this is easily one of your greatest strengths, you must work to avoid an obsession with power that will negatively affect your personal relationships. You social skills allow you to manipulate others to your advantage. While this skill is appealing, attempt to embrace your compassion to maintain healthy relationships.
